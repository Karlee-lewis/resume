Hobbies

"You mean like, Monopoly? Or Clue?" -Most People

I have recently been lured into the world of board games (let's face it, someone showed me like two games and I fell in love). My collection is growing and I have definitely gone all in. I find myself buying the organizers and spending hours sanding/staining them, but I love when the base set box accomodates expansions. Some of my favorite board games include: 7 wonders Duel, Valeria Card Kingdoms, and Clank!

I would love to venture into Table Top RPG's and have amassed waaaaay too many source books. I'm most excited by DND 5e, Pendragon, and Legend of the Five Rings. I enjoy painting minis and have really enjoyed building model buildings and terrain. It's safe to say I have a pile of minis and projects to paint at any given moment.

"Never tell me the odds!"- Han Solo, Empire Strikes Back

I guess you could say I am a nerd.

I love video games (Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Mass Effect). I love Star Wars (I accept the superiority of the Original Trilogy, but I'm filled with prequel memes). I adore the MCU, The Lord of the Rings, and even delving into comic books. I'm a fan of Image comics (Saga, Descender, Spawn), and generally prefer Marvel (X-men, Deadpool, Spiderman) over DC (I have a fondness for Batman).

"I know my value. Anyone else's opinion doesn't really matter"- Peggy Carter, Agent Carter

I was fortunate in my life to have been surrounded by art and artistic people from a very young age. I have fond memories as a child drawing, painting, and learning how to make pottery from clay. Learning how to tie dye, sew, and explore life through the lens of art. I can usually be found having several projects going at once in various mediums as well. My favorites are acrylic on canvas or board, pen and ink, and watercolor.
